FAQ

1. How can I access the irc server?
  • Configure your preferred IRC program such as mIRC or XCHAT (we prefer mIRC) with the following server settings:
  • IRC Server: irc.toonami.net
  • Port: 6667 (default IRC port)
  • Main Channel: #toonami
  • Register by first setting your desired nick Example:( /nick UserNameYouWant ) and then (/nickserv register YouDesiredPassword YourEmailAdress)
  • Login by first setting your desired nick Example:( /nick UserNameYouWant ) and then (/identify YourPassword)

2. How can I reset my account password?

  • Ask a moderator to 'drop' your username. Better solution coming soon.

3. How do I report a problem or a bug?

  • In chat, click on user toonamiaft and message any details so that we are aware of the problem. All too often bugs go unannounced.

4. What do I do if I'm banned for rule5a, multiple user accounts?

  • type /logout in order to logout of the offending username and proceed to login with your usual or most commonly used username for this chat.

5. How can I logout of chat?

  • type /logout in chat

6. Is Toonami Aftermath affiliated with Cartoon Network?

  • Toonami Aftermath is a nonprofit fan site which only goal is to bring older Toonami fans together.
